# product vision
SuperCli optimizes focus on creating business value by providing the tools, configuration, access and connectivity developers need to do their job. It facilitates standardization and fosters innovation, creativity and productivity among developers, while also mitigating operational and cyber risks.

## Target Groups
- Software development teams
- Product roles
- Security engineers

## Needs
- Availability of tools that are needed to create business value.
- Seamless need-to-have secure connectivity.
- Balanced user experience & risk mitigation.
- Up-and-running on the first day.
- Reduction of cognitive load.

## Features
- Inner-source means to provision connectivity.
- Inner-source means to provisioning of generic toolset & configuration.

## Business goals
- Reduce time-to-productivity 
  - 4 hours for new employees
  - one hour for a laptop switch
- Improvement DevEx rating by 10%
- Decline of used workarounds by 75%
